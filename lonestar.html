Name: Joshua Blackstone

Age: 20
Birth date: November 1
Nationality: French
Ethnicity: British
Height: 6'0"/182.7cm
Eye/Hair color: Dark Brown

Likes: Chocolate milkshakes, spaghetti bolognese, orange candies, tea cakes, Helen Pomeroy
Dislikes: Arrogant/intolerant people, gossip, large bodies of water, Gregory Halbert
Occupation: Successor to Blackstone Arms weapons development corporation, currently a university student at Von Bromsonn Academy
Hobbies: Hunting, fencing, piano, video games, world domination through use of Lego
Joshua Blackstone is the son of Alistair and Adella Blackstone, and younger brother to Jacob. Joshua is a quiet, polite young man known for his dry wit who keeps mostly to himself except when dealing with work or close friends. His parents died when he was 10 years old, and shortly afterward, Jacob left to study space law abroad. Young Joshua moved to Mont Saint-Michel, France to live with his Uncle Gerald Blackstone and his cousin Madeline (and later his young cousin Alex.) Since living in France, Joshua has grown to learn managing his Uncle's corporation, Blackstone Arms, and is in line to succeed as CEO. Along with his uncle, he is trying to secure a more permanent financial relationship with Giorgio Vorianos' corporation, YM Industries. He currently studies at Von Bromsonn Academy on the Von Bromsonn Asteroid orbiting Luna. It is there that he befriends his colleague Mario Vorianos, heir to YM Industries. He is fiercely loyal to his friends and family, as he feels that life is short and precious. However, despite his outward confidence and politeness, he is incredibly introverted, and currently struggles with confessing his feelings of affection towards Helen Pomeroy, whom he has loved for many years.

Name: Liam Chambers

Age: 21
Birth date: August 15
Nationality: British
Ethnicity: British
Height: 6'0"/182.7cm
Eye/Hair color: light blue/brown, wears glasses

Likes: Pizza, seafood, chocolates, cute girls, gossip, animals (especially dogs)
Dislikes: Spicy food, losing, jerks
Occupation: Celebrity/actor, circus performer, currently a university student at Von Bromsonn Academy
Hobbies: Circus activities, parkour, video games, hunting
Liam Chambers is an enthusiastic, cheerful young man who comes from a family of performers. His parents worked various jobs to support their amateur acting career, setting the foundation for Liam to pursue a life of performance. He began performing in circus at an early age, including high-energy acts Fire Poi and Whipcracking. He took his love for performance and entertainment to the cinema in his late teens starring in the western movie thriller, "The Savage Coast" as the role of John West. Since then he has performed in various British TV dramas, most famously in "Raining Crime," where Liam currently plays the role of a dashing vigilante detective, John Bartelsby, when he is not busy with school work. Due to his busy schedule, he only has leisure time for the most important people in his life. His career has wreaked havoc on any chance for a long-term, stable, romantc relationship. However, this has not deterred any attention from his fanbase. Liam is Joshuaís best friend from England since childhood. Ever since Joshua moved at the age of 10 to live in France with his uncle, Gerald Blackstone, Liam has kept himself busy with performances and a somewhat healthy social life. Every summer he spends time with Joshua at the Blackstone estate at Mont Saint-Michel. He is a senior at Von Bromsonn Academy, a year ahead of Joshua, and meets Mario Vorianos after Joshua befriends him.

She is currently the object of Gregoryís infatuation, a source of contention for her as her feelings toward him are complicated.

Name: Mario Vorianos

Age: 21
Birth date: October 30
Nationality: Greek
Ethnicity: Greek
Height: 6'1"/185.3cm
Eye/Hair color: dark brown/dark brown-black, wears glasses

Likes: Cats (owns many cats), chicken souvlaki, spicy food, ouzo
Dislikes: Racism, liars, wasting money, being ignored
Occupation: Successor to YM Industries, currently a university student at Von Bromsonn Academybr> Hobbies: Fishing, camping, visiting ancient Greek ruins by motorcycle, robotics, video games
Mario Vorianos, son of Giorgio and Eleni Vorianos, heir to Ypermihanima Industries (YMI), is best known for his critical directness and lack of sugar-coating the truth. This has worked in his favor by keeping him from befriending those who would only serve to waste his time. He began to steel his nerves shortly after he experienced his first taste of war at the age of 3 when armored tanks began to patrol down the streets in Athens, Greece and destroyed the homes of his friends. Fleeing to a small island in the Mediterranean, Giorgio took his family and developed their agricultural robotics business, and lived successfully from then on. During his studies at Von Bromsonn Academy on the Lunar asteroid, Mario met Joshua Blackstone and became friends. Joshua quickly proposed the use of Mario's mechanical suits and robotics for weapons deployment, and since then has bolstered YM Industries profits. Along with Blackstone Arms, his company currently supplies half the world's military contracts in the ongoing World War. More recently, Mario has become engaged to Madeline Blackstone, Joshua's cousin, and seems rather apprehensive about it.

Likes: avocados, going into work at noon, making a killer profit, puns
Dislikes: Cauliflower, waking up early, flaky politicians (but that's a tautology)
Occupation: Chief Systems Engineer and co-owner at Lone Star Space Station/Mining Operations
Hobbies: Kitesurfing, hunting, robotics, weightlifting, fishing, staying up late
Knowing all his life that he loved space and robots, Randy began working at the Lone Star Mining Company along with his most trusted friend, Travis. After years of hard work, they have climbed the ranks and have practically inherited the privately-owned, Texas-based company. During the period known as the Asteroid Wars, Randy, against the wishes of Travis and others close to him, attempted to alter an incoming meteor to impact upon Washington D.C. However, he relented and the asteroid was captured into Lunar orbit, was later colonized, and became known as the Von Bromsonn Colony. Due to Randy's threat upon the capitol of the world, however, the heads of LSMC were effectively exiled from Earth and can no longer return, but must still pay taxes to the United Earth Government or face total embargo. These experiences have shaped his current self and his fierce work ethic. Although highly skeptical and slightly burned out, he retains his politeness as a tool for survival in the harsh vastness of space.

Name: Travis

Age: Undisclosed
Birth date: Undisclosed
Nationality: Texan
Ethnicity: Hawaiian
Height: 6'2"/187.8cm
Eye/Hair color: Brown/Brown

Likes: Batman, curry, Tex-mex food, coffee, danger
Dislikes: Freeloaders, hot foods (warm is ok), scotch tape, zombie apocalypses, pirates
Occupation: Chief AI Architecture Engineer and co-owner at Lone Star Space Station/Mining Operations
Hobbies: Kitesurfing, deep sea fishing, extreme endurance races, hunting, piano, marimba, drums
Known as an incredibly intelligent child, Travis graduated at an early age. Shortly after obtaining his Ph.D. in AI architecture in a little over 1 year, Travis wrote software to help automate processes in the oil drilling industry in West Texas. During the Asteroid Wars, he forged a close friendship with Randy while working together at the Lone Star Mining Company (LSMC). After being exiled from Earth following the successful capture of the Von Bromsonn Asteroid into Lunar orbit, they sought energy solutions in the space frontier. Aggressively opposed by various nations, Travis continued to work with Randy to gain worldwide investors for the LSMC they essentially owned by crowd-funded campaigns to fund their asteroid mining business. Due to their perseverance, they were able to improve upon mining operations by installing his unique A.I. system in their mining drones. Currently their main operations are located at the Lone Star Space Station (LSSS) positioned in Martian orbit. They lease out equipment and software to help third-party mining business operations and establish secure trading caravans across the solar system, as well as mining and refining materials obtained from captured asteroids themselves. Their success has allowed the continued homesteading and employment of thousands of people in space. Known as a cheerful workaholic, Travis brings alot of energy to his work and never stops thinking of new ideas and projects. He becomes very stressed if his programming results in unexpected consequences, and works endlessly to debug them. If you ever stop by his quarters, you'll get a better idea on how...industrious he is.
